## 四则运算规则

若 $\lim f(x) = A, \lim g(x) = b$，则：

1. $\lim [kf(x)\pm l\lim g(x)]=kA \pm lB$
2. $\lim [kf(x) \cdot g(x)]=A\cdot B$
3. 若 $\lim f(x)$ 存在，且 $n$ 为正整数，则 $\lim [f(x)]^n = [lim f(x)]^n$
4. $\lim \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{\lim f(x)}{\lim g(x)}=\frac{A}{B} (B \ne 0)$
