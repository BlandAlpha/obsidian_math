方程组：
$$
\left\{\begin{matrix} 
  a_{11}x_1 + a_{12} + \cdots + a_{1n}x_n = b_{1} \\  
  a_{21}x_1 + a_{22} + \cdots + a_{2n}x_n = b_{2} \\
  \cdots \\
  a_{m1}x_1 + a_{m2} + \cdots + a_{mn}x_n = b_{m}
\end{matrix}\right. 
$$

> [!note] 
> 等号右边换成 $b$ 向量

把解向量放进去可获得增广矩阵
